Our history

Founded in Florence, Italy, in 1928 by cobbler **Silvio Moretti**, Tiptoe Soles was born from a desire for elegance without noise. Moretti's innovation was the proprietary **"Tiptoe Insert,"** a layered cork compound that absorbed shock and silenced the sharp *clack* of traditional leather soles. This "Silent Step" became the company's signature.

The brand soared in popularity among actors and high-society women who valued discreet, graceful movement. The 1950s saw the release of the iconic **Tiptoe Ballet Flat**, cementing their place in fashion history.

Today, operating from Milan, Tiptoe Soles maintains its focus on specialized comfort. They are favored by professionals (like doctors and librarians) who spend long hours on their feet, alongside a luxury "Heritage" line. The company successfully bridges traditional Italian craftsmanship with modern sustainable materials, proving that quality and quiet innovation endure.
